Frequently Asked Questions

How does SmartBooks generate recommendations?

SmartBooks uses Content-Based Filtering, TF-IDF Vectorization and Cosine Similarity.

Do I need an account?

Yes. An account allows you to save favorites, rate books and receive personalized recommendations.

Is SmartBooks free?

Yes. SmartBooks is completely free to use.

How do I get better recommendations?

Add books to your favorites and rate books you have read. The more interactions you make, the more accurate your recommendations become.
